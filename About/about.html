About Us

Our story

Welcome to Clean Factory, where cleanliness meets quality. Established in 2019 in the heart of Zakho, Iraq, Clean Factory has quickly become a trusted name in household cleaning products. We take pride in manufacturing a wide range of high-quality cleaning solutions designed to meet the diverse needs of our customers.

Our Mission

At Clean Factory, our mission is simple: to provide effective, safe, and affordable cleaning products that make your home sparkle. We believe that a clean home is a happy home, and we are dedicated to helping our customers achieve the highest standards of cleanliness with ease.

Our Products

Our product line includes a variety of household cleaning essentials, such as: Detergents: Powerful and gentle formulas for all your laundry needs. Dishwashing Liquids: Tough on grease, gentle on hands. Hand Soaps: Effective hygiene solutions that keep your hands clean and soft. Whether you are looking for the perfect detergent to keep your clothes fresh, a dishwashing liquid that cuts through tough grease, or a hand soap that provides reliable hygiene, Clean Factory has you covered.

Our Approach

Quality is at the core of everything we do. Our products are crafted with the finest ingredients and undergo rigorous testing to ensure they meet the highest standards of performance and safety. We are committed to continuous innovation, always looking for new ways to enhance our product offerings and better serve our customers.

Our Commitment to the Community

We are proud to be a part of the Zakho community and are dedicated to giving back. Clean Factory supports local initiatives and strives to create job opportunities, fostering economic growth and development in the region.
